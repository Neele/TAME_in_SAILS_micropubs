Progressive –cha

Indirect knowlegde –mono

Perfective –a

Past tense –taah

Continuative –no. I think this is an imperfective as Simon states that it is I oposition to the prfective –a.

Declarative –te

 

Present tense is formed by the verbal root +-no+declarative+person marker

Perfective is formed by the verbal root + pfv suffix –a +aux ka-  + declarative –te + person marker

 Future –ra related to the verb dach ‘want’

Completive –hi (309)

 

Modality:

Two suffixes to express a possible event: -taycha and –ramcha (307, 320)

-nem marks an event that is very much probable (320)

 

Imperative sg: -a, imperative pl –noku, both second person (307). To form a imperative with third person, the imperative verb gets a prefix ka-
